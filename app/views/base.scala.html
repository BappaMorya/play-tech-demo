Thank Back - Say thanks to all Facebook birthday wishes in less than 10 seconds!

ThankBack - Step 1

Login using your Facebook account, ThankBack needs couple of permissions to access data like your birth-date (duh!)

After saying thanks to all wishes on your behalf, ThankBack gives back all permissions




ThankBack - Step 2

ThankBack detects birthday wishes from posts on your birthday

Whatever doesn't get detected automatically, you can decide if its a birthday wish or not!




ThankBack - Step 3

You get to decide how you want to say thanks - Like it or reply to a post

After saying thanks to all wishes, according to what you've decided!
